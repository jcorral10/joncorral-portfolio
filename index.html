About

Craft first. Always.

I'm Jon. I've spent over a decade figuring out how to make brands sound like themselves — and I'm still not tired of it. Great copy should feel like it was always there, just waiting to be found.

Right now I write for Hill's Pet Nutrition, one of the world's leading pet health brands. Social campaigns, email sequences, product pages, veterinary education — if it needs words, I'm probably in the doc.

What makes my process a little different? I've built AI into the craft — not to replace the writing, but to make the research faster, the first drafts smarter, and the QA sharper. The pen is still mine. The ink just flows a little smarter now.

The quiet advantage

AI sharpens the craft.
It doesn't do the writing.

I didn't learn AI to stop writing. I learned it to make every stage of the process more precise, more informed, and frankly — more fun. So the writing itself can be more human.

Knowledge Bases

Custom AI knowledge bases for brand voice, product data, and regulatory guidelines — so the AI knows the brand's rules as well as I do.

Prompt-Engineered Templates

Purpose-built prompts that generate on-brand first drafts — not generic outputs, but structured starting points tuned to specific deliverables.

Automated QA

Claims review, tone consistency checks, and compliance validation — automated to catch issues before stakeholders see the draft.

Workflow Integration

From assignment intake to final delivery — connected systems that reduce friction and keep the creative momentum unbroken.

The best tools disappear into the work. AI doesn't make me a better writer — it makes me a faster, sharper one. And that means more time for the part I actually love: the ideas.
